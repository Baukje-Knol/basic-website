New Zealand, known as 'Aotearoa' in the native Maori language is a dream destination for many world travellers. The natural beauty of the country answers to the needs of both the adventurer and the one looking for a relaxing getaway.

If you're looking for a challenge which tests your boundaries, consider bungee jumping, hiking the fjords, surfing, skiing or white-water rafting.

 Looking to wind down and reboot to the backdrop of the serene nature that this country has to offer? There are numerous walking trails which take you past breathtaking scenery with only the sound of birds and streams to accompany your meditations.
